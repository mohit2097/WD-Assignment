Who I Am

I am Mohit, a passionate individual born on April 29, 2004. My identity is shaped by a love for learning and a keen interest in the world around me. When I'm not engrossed in my studies, I find solace in reading books and embarking on journeys of self-improvement by acquiring new skills. I thrive in the company of my joint family, cherishing the bonds that tie us together.
